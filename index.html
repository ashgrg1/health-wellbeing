Medication Goals

These lifestyle improvements may, over time, allow your doctor to reduce medications:

Pioglitazone (Diabetes)
Diet control, exercise, weight loss — A1C below 6.5 could reduce need
Lisinopril (Blood Pressure)
Low sodium, exercise, weight loss, stress reduction — BP <120/80 sustained
Atorvastatin (Cholesterol)
Plant-based diet, oats, exercise — LDL below 100 consistently
Levothyroxine / Liothyronine (Thyroid)
Selenium, zinc, iodine-rich foods help thyroid. TSH must be monitored. Rarely eliminated.
Divalproex / Oxcarbazepine (Mood)
Sleep consistency, stress reduction, therapy — always reduce with psychiatrist only
